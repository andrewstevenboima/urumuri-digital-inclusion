## 🎯 Project Context

**Problem:** Children with ADHD in rural Rwanda have a 40% higher dropout risk. Schools lack culturally relevant tools and teachers are undertrained in ADHD support.

**Solution:** Urumuri delivers gamified, sensory-friendly micro-learning modules in Kinyarwanda, with a real-time teacher dashboard and embedded ADHD professional development guides.

**Pilot:** 20 rural schools in Bugesera District, targeting 200 students.

**Impact Targets:**
- 25–35% increase in student engagement
- 20% improvement in task completion rates  
- 70% teacher satisfaction rating
- 15–20% reduction in dropout risk among ADHD students
